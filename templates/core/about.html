Doctor Svetozar Drenski

Biography

"Dr. Svetozar Drenski is a specialist dermatologist in Sofia with over 25 years of experience. Performs examinations, consultations and diagnostics of patients with skin and venereal diseases of various nature. His interests are mainly focused on the treatment of rosacea and vitiligo. He graduated from the Medical University of Sofia in 1992 and obtained a specialty in Dermatology and Venereology after specialization at the Clinic of Prof. Stoyan Tonev at the Sofia Military Medical Academy. Additional qualifications Participation in numerous national congresses and seminars on the specialty. From 1997 to the present, Dr. Drenski has an independent practice as a specialist dermatologist in Sofia. In addition to dermatology, since 1995 he has been engaged in the import of medical cosmetics and nutritional supplements."
