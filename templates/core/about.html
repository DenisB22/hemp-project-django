Who are we?

We are a group of highly motivated members. Our goal is to educate people about the positive effects of Cannabinoid (CBD).

Let us introduce you to the core of our team:

Biography

Dr. Svetozar Drenski is a specialist dermatologist in Sofia with over 25 years of experience. Performs examinations, consultations and diagnostics of patients with skin and venereal diseases of various nature. His interests are mainly focused on the treatment of rosacea and vitiligo. He graduated from the Medical University of Sofia in 1992 and obtained a specialty in Dermatology and Venereology after specialization at the Clinic of Prof. Stoyan Tonev at the Sofia Military Medical Academy. Additional qualifications Participation in numerous national congresses and seminars on the specialty. From 1997 to the present, Dr. Drenski has an independent practice as a specialist dermatologist in Sofia. In addition to dermatology, since 1995 he has been engaged in the import of medical cosmetics and nutritional supplements.


And here is another representative of our team:

Biography

Denis Bechiragich is a 25-year-old Software Developer and entrepreneur from Montenegro. He was born in the Balkans and has spent most of his life in Bulgaria. After completing high school, Denis pursued opportunities abroad and worked and studied in Vienna, Austria. During his time in Vienna, Denis held various positions in the hospitality industry, eventually becoming a supervisor at the Moxy Hotel at the Vienna International Airport. However, he realized that his true passion lay in programming, and decided to pursue a career in that field. In addition to his work as a Software Developer and his occasional lectures at a university, Denis also has a successful business venture. He saw a market opportunity in the growing CBD industry while living in Vienna, and decided to bring his vision back to Bulgaria. In August 2022, he and his parents opened the first physical CBD shop in their hometown of Plovdiv. Denis is dedicated to helping others improve their quality of life through the use of CBD products. A fun fact: this website is created by him for a Software Academy where he studied as his final project.
